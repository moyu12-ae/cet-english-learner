### 1\. The Irregular at Magic High School (Spring 2014)

Magic Anime The Irregular at Magic High School

Magic is no longer a product of legends or fairy tales; it has been a reality for nearly a century. Tatsuya Shiba, an older brother with a certain “defect,” and his flawless younger sister, Miyuki, enroll at the National Magic University Affiliated First High School. From the moment they arrive, their peaceful days of learning turn into a whirlwind of turbulence.

### 2\. The Legend of the Legendary Heroes (Summer 2010)

![densetsu no yuusha no densetsu the legend of the legendary v0 6iifhxgayiqe1](https://dailyanimenews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/densetsu-no-yuusha-no-densetsu-the-legend-of-the-legendary-v0-6iifhxgayiqe1.webp)

densetsu no yuusha no densetsu the legend of the legendary v0 6iifhxgayiqe1

Ryner Lute, a student at the Roland Empire Royal Special Academy, is a lethargic underachiever who wants nothing more than to nap the days away. However, his life is upended when the neighboring Estabul Kingdom declares war. Ryner and his classmates are sent to the front lines, where he tragically loses many of his friends. After the war, he embarks on a journey with Sion to save Roland, but a looming darkness begins to consume the empire itself.

### 3\. Fairy Tail (Fall 2009)

![e94691913586a41106c0c1a6ab59cb7c1c9049ef9a7969b5036b8c23fdc42919](https://dailyanimenews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/e94691913586a41106c0c1a6ab59cb7c1c9049ef9a7969b5036b8c23fdc42919-2048x1152.jpg)

Countless wizard guilds exist across the globe, serving as agencies where magic users find work. Lucy, a young girl aspiring to become a full-fledged wizard, has a fateful encounter with Natsu, a Dragon Slayer who eats, breathes, and fights with fire. She joins his guild, “Fairy Tail,” only to discover it is filled with rowdy troublemakers far beyond her wildest imagination. Despite the chaos, Lucy and Natsu successfully complete their quests, steadily growing as formidable wizards along the way.

### 4\. Blast of Tempest (Fall 2012)

![1737975053891 i 1](https://dailyanimenews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/1737975053891-i-1.avif)


One day, a sorceress princess is exiled to a deserted island where her magic is nullified. On another day, a young boy makes a pact with her in the name of revenge. Thus begins an unprecedented, global battle woven entirely from vengeance and magic.

### 5\. Overlord (Summer 2015)

![G69PZ5PDY backdrop wide](https://dailyanimenews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/G69PZ5PDY-backdrop_wide.avif)


The year is 2138. The highly immersive virtual reality game *YGGDRASIL*, which once defined an era of gaming, is scheduled to quietly shut down. However, when the server closing time passes, players are not logged out. NPCs suddenly awaken with their own consciousness, and beyond the guild’s walls lies a completely unknown, fantastical world. In reality, the protagonist is just a lonely, unremarkable young man who loves games. Now, transformed into Momonga, the supreme skeletal wizard, he sets out to forge the legend of his guild, Ainz Ooal Gown!

### 6\. Magi: The Labyrinth of Magic (Fall 2012)

![AAAABYSTFkP6rwqBq5 tVW XFs2IdCqwq24k5b1dX2bXTLqQqQNI6SHdbHS3 R5bBZ4wGKv mu1VjLi 0JdKyNdr3d9JY1Th4nniOU7P](https://dailyanimenews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/AAAABYSTFkP6rwqBq5-tVW-XFs2IdCqwq24k5b1dX2bXTLqQqQNI6SHdbHS3_R5bBZ4wGKv-mu1VjLi_0JdKyNdr3d9JY1Th4nniOU7P.jpg)


Aladdin is a young boy traveling across a mysterious continent where magical ruins known as “Dungeons” suddenly appear, said to hold incredible treasures. Along his journey, he befriends Alibaba, and together they aim to conquer a Dungeon and claim its riches. Through a series of profound encounters and heartbreaking farewells, Aladdin slowly uncovers the ultimate truth of his own destiny.

### 7\. IRODUKU: The World in Colors (Fall 2018)

![9c5925caa5cbce8a2dfcc2e116bf802a86c6595bf1ece89c26c5f658fd09f25c](https://dailyanimenews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/9c5925caa5cbce8a2dfcc2e116bf802a86c6595bf1ece89c26c5f658fd09f25c.jpg)

The story begins decades in the future in Nagasaki, a slightly wondrous world where small traces of magic still exist in everyday life. The protagonist, 17-year-old Hitomi Tsukishiro, is the descendant of a lineage of mages. Having lost her sense of color at a young age, she grew up emotionally distant. Worried about her future, her grandmother, the great mage Kohaku, uses her magic to send Hitomi back in time to the year 2018. Suddenly thrust into an unfamiliar place, Hitomi is overwhelmed as vivid, brilliant colors flood her vision once more.

### 8\. Fate/Zero (Fall 2011)

![881f9f6ede21469da07b04bc2f7cc69144a5f20fda4339b3251761028cd6e95b](https://dailyanimenews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/881f9f6ede21469da07b04bc2f7cc69144a5f20fda4339b3251761028cd6e95b.jpg)

This is the story of the beginning—the “Zero.” The Holy Grail War is the ultimate battle royale where seven Mages (Masters) summon seven Heroic Spirits (Servants) to fight until only one remains, all to claim the miracle-granting Holy Grail. After being postponed three times, the fourth conflict is finally ignited. Mages flock to the battlefield of Fuyuki, each carrying their own desperate wish for victory. Yet, among them stands one man who finds no meaning in the fight: Kirei Kotomine. Unable to understand the hand fate has dealt him, Kirei wanders and questions why the Command Seals were bestowed upon him. Eventually, the brutal destiny of the war pits him against his fated nemesis: Kiritsugu Emiya, a man more relentless and ruthless than any other in his pursuit of the miraculous Grail.

### 9\. Saga of Tanya the Evil (Winter 2017)

![GR9P57W96 backdrop wide](https://dailyanimenews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/GR9P57W96-backdrop_wide.avif)

GR9P57W96 backdrop wide

June 1923. Tanya Degurechaff, a young girl with blonde hair and blue eyes, is diligently undergoing her final training at the Imperial Military Academy. Stationed at the third patrol line in the Norden Theater, her training is meant to be the safe, uneventful first step toward a glorious career as an aerial mage. However, border transgressions by the Legadonia Entente Alliance trigger a full-scale war with the Empire. Trapped in an ambush by an enemy mage company and hopelessly outnumbered, Tanya faces execution for desertion if she flees. Determined to survive and prove her worth to her superiors, Tanya executes a daring, reckless strategy. *She is a monster wearing the skin of a little girl.*

### 10\. A Certain Magical Index (Fall 2008)

![a certain magical index season 2 collection anime reviews art banner](https://dailyanimenews.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/a-certain-magical-index-season-2-collection-anime-reviews-art-banner.jpg)

a certain magical index season 2 collection anime reviews art banner

In a world where supernatural abilities are entirely explained by science, a massive Academy City integrates power development directly into its school curriculum. Touma Kamijo, a high school student living in this city, crosses paths with a young girl dressed in a pure white nun’s habit. She introduces herself as “Index” and claims she is currently being hunted by dangerous sorcerers. With this sudden encounter, Touma inadvertently steps out of the safe world of science and into the shadowy, volatile realm of the occult.
